Who we are

Built on a decade
of doing it.

Zorro Studios is an Australian games studio formed by the founding team behind GoGallop Studios — creators of FEI Equestriad World Tour, a globally licensed equestrian mobile game that reached millions of downloads and came within weeks of an IPO on the Canadian Securities Exchange.

We have taken everything learned from that venture — the licensing model, the sponsorship integration, the fan engagement mechanics — and built a new vehicle designed to move faster and think bigger.

Our model is direct: partner with a sport that has an underserved digital audience, control the IP relationship, and build a platform — not a single title — that compounds in value over time.

"The strategic question is no longer whether a sport should participate in gaming — but whether it should control the platform that defines its fan economy."

Zorro Studios · Strategic Framework 2026

Our approach

The platform playbook.

01
License the sport

Secure an official licensing relationship with the governing body or league. Real venues, real athletes, real competition structures. Authenticity is the only thing that converts fans into players.

02
Control the platform

Rather than handing IP to a third-party publisher, we structure deals so the sport retains control. The platform becomes a compounding digital asset — not a one-off licensing fee.

03
Integrate sponsorship

In-game sponsorship mirrors the real world — digital signage, branded kit, leaderboards, product placement. Partners reach a live audience. The game stays free to play.

04
Build the flywheel

Fans become players. Players become competitors. Competitors become viewers. Viewers attract commercial partners. Each turn strengthens every other part of the ecosystem.

05
Expand the universe

Mobile-first. Then PC and console. Then esports. The first title is proof of concept. The platform is designed to evolve — new modes, new seasons, new sports.

06
Partner with capital

We structure partnerships with listed entities who bring governance, market access, and publishing scale — reducing execution risk while accelerating speed to market.

Fans → Players → Competitors → Viewers → Commercial Partners
